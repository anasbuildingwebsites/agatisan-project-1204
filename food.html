Nasi Lemak Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 pandan leaves (tied into a knot)
  • Salt to taste
  • Sambal (spicy chili paste)
  • Fried anchovies (ikan bilis)
  • Roasted peanuts
  • Boiled eggs
  • Sliced cucumber

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and drain.
  2. In a rice cooker, add rice, coconut milk, water, pandan leaves, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Cook until rice is soft and fragrant.
  4. Meanwhile, prepare sambal by blending chilies, onions, and garlic. Saut√© and season with sugar, salt, and tamarind paste.
  5. Fry anchovies until crispy and roast the peanuts separately.
  6. Boil eggs, peel, and halve them.
  7. Serve the coconut rice with sambal, anchovies, peanuts, boiled eggs, and cucumber slices.

Tip: Wrap everything in banana leaves for extra aroma and an authentic Malaysian touch!
